About Macista Cybercafe

Welcome to Macista, where cutting-edge tech meets cozy community vibes! We’re more than just an internet café—we’re a hub for gamers, digital nomads, and creatives. Founded in 2021, our mission is to provide top-tier gaming rigs, blazing-fast internet, and a welcoming space to work, play, and connect.
From high-octane esports to casual browsing, we’ve got you covered with premium PCs, consoles, and even printing services.

KRA & E-Citizen Services

Skip the queues! At Macista, we help with KRA tax filings (iTax, PIN updates), NTSA services (DL renewal, logbook checks), NHIF/NSSF registration, and other e-Citizen transactions. Bring your ID and visit us today for stress-free government services!
